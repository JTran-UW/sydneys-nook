Sydney's Nook

A room of my own

The Nook Opens

June 20, 2022

I resolved to start writing. Every day, every week, every year--it didn’t matter, just write. The only thing I knew that would keep me accountable was publicity, an audience. Just knowing one person could happen upon my internet pitstop keeps the words flowing from my little desk in Seattle.

The subline is a reference to Virgina Woolf’s novel A Room of One’s Own. It concerns a woman’s need for independent space--physical, mental, emotional--to write and express herself. In this digital world, I figure a website, an online room of my own, only makes sense.
